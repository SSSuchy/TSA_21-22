FAQ

We are a group of high school students who want to make esports and competition more easy to access. With the current state of technology, and the plethora of ways to get connected online, fully online competitions are viable and we want to make them accessible for other high school students or younger competitors.

The Retake tournaments are not part of any bigger circuit, so no, there are no prior requirements to sign up. While mostly for recreational purposes, we also make the tournaments open so that people can invite their friends to compete regardless, making the whole experience more fun.

We do, but the process is slow. The main reason we can't offer games like CS:GO is because it would be nearly impossible to allow high schools to work with us for an official event if the game is violent or has non-school appropriate themes. This is the current state of Apex and Valorant. Of course, these games can be played in non-collaboration tournaments, just for fun in the Retake Discord.

As of right now, the Apex, Rocket League, and Super Smash Bros. Ultimate tournaments are all free. The Valorant tournament is 5$ per player, or 25 per team, because it follows a format similar to real pro tournaments that guarantees several matches for each team.

Yes, there are prizes for the grand winner of the Apex, Rocket League, and Super Smash Bros. Ultimate tournaments. The Valorant tournament will give some prizes to all players, depending on their finish.

You can watch matches on the Retake_US twitch channel and Discord server. We also host Zoom meetings for Discord members who are interested in working behind the scenes in esports - casting and commentating, editing or observing, or analyzing pro matches.
