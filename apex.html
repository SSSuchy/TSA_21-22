Diabetes Management

Specialized Programs for any Diabetes

Omega Health also provides our diabetic patients and better ways to monitor their condition. With our Dieticians and Endocrinologists, we are able to help you track your progress and manage your blood sugar. We send you our care package for this plan and allow you to use our app to track your diet. Once a week our team will meet with you one on one virtually and help analyze your data and suggest a better plan for you. We are very welcoming and anyone regardless of age can use this specialized plan.

Team of Dieticians and Endocrinologists

Our comprehensive specialized team will help anyone regardless of previous conditions and allow you to help set your own diet and analyze your data.

  • Personalized Diet plan to help lower cholesterol and blood sugar
  • Weekly checkups with your dietician to better improve your condition
  • Care Package included with goods to better record your blood concentration
  • Personalized graphs and charts for better comprehension of your condition & improvement
